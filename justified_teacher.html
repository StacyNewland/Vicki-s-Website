Teaching


After earning my BS in English from East Carolina University, I quickly began teaching and earned my MA in English as well.


My journey as an English teacher began in Craven County, at West Craven High School, then West Craven Junior High School. After moving to Raleigh, I worked in the Wake County Public School System. I taught at Broughton High School, Enloe High School, and Leesville Road Middle School.
